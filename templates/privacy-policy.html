WebDocEdit Privacy Policy

Effective Date: 2024/07/20

1. Introduction

At WebDocEdit, your privacy and data security are important to us. This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and your rights regarding your data.

2. Information We Collect

  • Log Data: We automatically collect information such as your IP address, browser type, and visit details to analyze trends and improve our service.
  • Personal Information: We may collect personal details like your name, email, and address when you register, make a purchase, or contact us. This information is collected with your consent and used to provide and enhance our services.

3. Use of Data

We use your data to:
  • Provide and maintain our services
  • Respond to your inquiries and fulfill requests
  • Send service-related updates and promotional communications (if you consent)

4. Sharing Your Data

We do not sell or share your personal information with third parties. We do not rely on external services for data processing, and your data is not shared outside of WebDocEdit.

5. Cookies

WebDocEdit uses cookies to enhance your browsing experience. You can manage or disable cookies through your browser settings.

6. Data Protection Rights

If you are a European resident, you have the right to access, correct, or delete your personal information. Contact us if you have questions about your rights or need assistance.

7. Data Retention

We keep your data only as long as necessary to provide our services or as required by law. Your data is protected against unauthorized access and misuse.

8. Email Communication

We may contact you via email for service-related purposes or with your consent for promotional updates. You can opt-out of these communications at any time.

9. Third-Party Links

Our site may contain links to other websites. We are not responsible for the privacy practices of these sites. Review their privacy policies before providing any personal information.

10. Advertisements

Our site may display ads from partners who may use cookies or web beacons. This policy does not cover third-party advertisers.

11. Children’s Privacy

Our service is not intended for individuals under 18. By using our site, you confirm that you are at least 18 years old.

12. Do Not Track

We do not alter our data collection practices in response to Do Not Track signals from your browser.

13. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page. Your continued use of our site constitutes acceptance of any updates.

14. Consent

By using WebDocEdit, you consent to our Privacy Policy and its terms.
